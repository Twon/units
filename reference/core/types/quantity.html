A quantity.

Property of a phenomenon, body, or substance, where the property has a magnitude that can be expressed by means of a number and a measurement unit.

tparam D

a dimension of the quantity (can be either a BaseDimension or a DerivedDimension)

tparam U

a measurement unit of the quantity

tparam Rep

a type to be used to represent values of a quantity
